Snowcap Admin Bundle
====================

Introduction
------------

SnowcapAdminBundle provides a few useful features for your backoffice applications. The goal of this bundle is to provide a series of tools and helpers to facilitate your work, but also to get out of your way when you want to add totally custom features.
